Poke Recipe

Home

Description

This poke recipe is a extremely simple recipe that takes less than 30 minutes to take. It is a delicious meal that uses a small list of ingredients that are appetizing and filling. A tasty and short meal to fulfill a craving!

Ingredients

  • Ahi Tuna
  • Green Onions
  • Sesame Seeds
  • White Rice
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Oil
  • Vinegar
  • Any vegetable of choice

Steps

  1. Mix soy sauce, vinegar, and sesame oil into a bowl.
  2. Add green onions and sesame seeds into the bowl.
  3. Cube the ahi tuna.
  4. Marinate the ahi tuna in the poke sauce.
  5. Refrigerate the ahi tuna for 15 mins.
  6. Dish the ahi tuna with the white rice.
  7. Add any vegetable option of choice, such as diced cucumber, seaweed salad, corn, etc.
