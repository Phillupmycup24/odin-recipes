Chicken Recipe

Home

Description

This is a delicious chicken recipe! It's quick to make since it uses only seasoning. It uses a variety of seasonings and is great for a healthy diet!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Dried Parsley
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Smoked Paprika

Steps

  1. Mix all the seasonings and oil in a bowl.
  2. Add the chicken and let it marinate for 15 minutes.
  3. Cook until internal temperature is at least 165 Fahrenheit or until golden brown.
